About Us

Our Suicide Risk Prediction Application is designed to detect potential suicidal tendencies and emotions in real-time Twitter posts. By analyzing the text content of tweets, the system identifies emotions such as sadness, anger, fear, or hopelessness—signals that may indicate someone is at risk of self-harm or mental distress.

Purpose

Suicide is a critical global issue, and early intervention can save lives. Our system leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning to monitor and evaluate emotional indicators in tweets, helping health professionals, crisis centers, and mental health researchers identify individuals who might need help—before it's too late.

Who is this application for?

  • Mental health professionals
  • Crisis response organizations and helplines
  • Researchers and analysts in mental health domains
  • Policy makers and NGOs focusing on mental well-being
  • Family members or concerned friends monitoring loved ones

Key Features

  • Upload tweet datasets to predict suicide risk levels
  • Emotion classification to understand user sentiment
  • Simple, user-friendly interface with secure login
  • Real-time alerts and insights for proactive response

Our Vision

We aim to support global mental health initiatives through technology—creating tools that not only analyze but also inspire action, compassion, and timely support.
